# Duncedmaxxing

Survival Hunter Tip of the Spear Tracker.

## Tip of the Spear

The tracker predicts your Tip of the Spear stacks from your own successful spellcasts, capped at 3:

- **Kill Command** (`259489`) grants +2 stacks and refreshes the 10-second window.
- **Takedown** (`1250646`) with Twin Fangs grants +3 then consumes 1 (net +2); without Twin Fangs it just consumes 1.
- Consumers spend 1 stack each: **Raptor Strike** (`186270`, `265189` under Aspect of the Eagle), **Raptor Swipe** (`1262293`, `1262343` under Aspect of the Eagle), **Wildfire Bomb** (`259495`), and **Boomstick** (`1261193`).

## Settings

Use `/dmax` (alias `/duncedmaxxing`) to open the movable settings window. All configuration is done there:

- display mode: segmented bar or plain stack number
- enabled and hide-when-empty visibility
- position, scale, bar width, height, border size, and number size
- fill color, border color, text color, and empty-segment opacity
- lock/unlock, preview, reset position, and reset style
